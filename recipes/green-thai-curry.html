Green Thai Curry

Description

This recipe is an authentic green thai curry, with chicken, eggplant and bamboo shoots.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup jasmine rice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 pound chicken tenders
  • 1 tbsp green curry paste
  • 1 tbsp fresh basil
  • 1 eggplant
  • 2/3 carton chicken broth
  • 1 can light coconut milk
  • 1 can bamboo shoots
  • 2 tsp lime juice
  • 1 small bunch fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Bring water and rice to a boil in a saucepan. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender and water has been asborbed, 20-25 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Mix in onion, fish sauce,brown sugar,ginger,and garlic; cook and stir about 5 minutes. Add chicken,curry paste, and basil. Cook,stirring occasionally,about 5 minutes more. Add egglant,cover, and reduce heat to low to avoid burning spices. Cook about 5 minutes more.
  3. Pour broth, coconut milk, and bamboo shoots into the saucepan. Continue cooking until eggplant is tender and curry is heated through, 5 to 10 minutes more. Stir in lime juice before serving over cooked rice with basil leaves.
